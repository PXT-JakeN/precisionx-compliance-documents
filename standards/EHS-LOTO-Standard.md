## 1. Purpose

Define lockout/tagout requirements to prevent injuries and service disruption during work on energized
equipment.

## 2. Scope

Applies to electrical and mechanical systems at NOCs and GridColo sites where maintenance creates hazards.

## 3. Control Requirements

| Req ID | Requirement | Implementation | Evidence |
|--------|-------------|----------------|----------|
| LOTO-01 | Authorized personnel | Only trained, authorized staff perform LOTO; maintain roster | Training logs; roster |
| LOTO-02 | Procedures | Document device‑specific LOTO steps and verification tests | Procedures; checklists |
| LOTO-03 | Devices | Use standardized locks/tags; one device per worker; supervisor oversight | Inventory; issue logs |
| LOTO-04 | Verification | Verify zero energy state before work; record results | Test records |
| LOTO-05 | Restoration | Controlled re‑energization with area sweep and notification | Restoration logs |
| LOTO-06 | Incidents | Report near‑misses/incidents; corrective actions tracked | Incident reports |

## 4. Roles and Responsibilities

Facilities manages program and training; supervisors enforce compliance; workers follow procedures and report
hazards; SecOps coordinates if service impact exists.
