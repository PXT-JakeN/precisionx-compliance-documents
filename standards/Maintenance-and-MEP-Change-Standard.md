## 1. Purpose

Define methods of procedure (MOP/MOPs) and controls for maintenance and mechanical/electrical/plumbing
changes at facilities to prevent outages and ensure safety.

## 2. Scope

Facility systems at NOCs and GridColo sites, including power distribution, cooling, fire suppression, and
network rooms.

## 3. Control Requirements

| Req ID | Requirement | Implementation | Evidence |
|--------|-------------|----------------|----------|
| MEP-01 | Planning | Document MOP with impact/risk, hold points, and rollback | MOP docs |
| MEP-02 | Approvals | Apply PR07; require facility management approval and stakeholder notice | Tickets; approvals |
| MEP-03 | LOTO & safety | Apply EHS/LOTO steps and PPE requirements | LOTO records; checklists |
| MEP-04 | Change window | Schedule during maintenance windows; NOC coordination | Schedules; comms |
| MEP-05 | Monitoring | Monitor during/after change; record anomalies | Logs; trend charts |
| MEP-06 | Restoration & rollback | Execute rollback if acceptance criteria fail | Logs; incident records |
| MEP-07 | Documentation | Update diagrams/asâ€‘built docs; store evidence | Diagrams; evidence repo |

## 4. Roles and Responsibilities

Facilities drafts MOPs and leads execution; NOC coordinates; SecOps/IT provide monitoring and incident
support.
