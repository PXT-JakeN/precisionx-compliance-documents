## 1. Purpose

Provide an auditable record of production changes, approvals, and outcomes.

## 2. Required Fields

Change ID; type (standard/normal/emergency); description; affected systems; risk rating; approvals; implementation
window; results; rollback executed (Y/N); post‑implementation review notes; evidence link.

## 3. Maintenance

Entries created from the change system of record; emergency changes logged within 24 hours; export evidence if change
platform cannot forward.
