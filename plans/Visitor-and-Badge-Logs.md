## 1. Purpose

Maintain auditable logs of visitor entries/exits, escorts, badge issuance/activation, and deactivations across sites.

## 2. Required Fields

Person; organization; visit type (tenant/vendor/guest/employee); escort (if required); sign‑in/out times; areas
authorized; temporary/permanent badge ID; three‑factor kiosk authentication events (badge/face/PIN); guard terminal
fallback events; lost/forgotten badge handling; appeals; evidence links. Export manually if kiosk/portal cannot forward.

## 3. Cadence

Real‑time capture with monthly review and anomaly sampling; retain per policy and legal obligations.
