## 1. Objectives

Increase knowledge of policies, reduce risky behaviors, and improve detection and reporting of phishing and
social engineering attempts.

## 2. Scope & Audience

All employees and contractors with role‑based modules for developers, admins, NOC, and facilities staff.

## 3. Program Components

- Onboarding: Core ISMS, classification, remote work, and incident reporting modules.
- Annual refresher: Updated threats, policy changes, and recent incidents.
- Role‑based training: SSDLC for developers; PAM and logging for admins; physical security for NOC/facilities.
- Phishing program: Periodic simulations with progressive difficulty; just‑in‑time training for clickers.
- Communications: Monthly tips and policy highlights.

## 4. Metrics & Reporting

Track completion rates, simulation results, and targeted coaching outcomes. Report metrics quarterly to
Management Review and include corrective actions where needed.

## 5. Schedule

Define annual calendar with quarterly phishing waves and monthly communications; adjust cadence based on
risk and performance.
