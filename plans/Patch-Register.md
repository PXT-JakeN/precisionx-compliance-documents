## 1. Purpose

Track patch availability, applicability, risk, approvals, and deployment status across systems and applications.

## 2. Required Fields

Patch ID/bulletin; vendor; affected products/versions; priority/risk; change ticket; maintenance window; approvals;
deployment status; deferrals/exceptions; verification results; evidence link.

## 3. Maintenance

Update weekly in line with patch cycles; tie entries to change records; export evidence if tooling cannot forward to the
repository.
