## 1. Overview

Design reference for primary and secondary NOCs, including roles separation, resiliency, and comms.

## 2. Architecture & Roles

Dual‑NOC with role separation (incident command vs. facilities coordination), 24×7 Teams AV link for a common operating
picture, and surge participation for additional staff during major events.

## 3. Facilities & Technology

Redundant power/network, segmented management networks, restricted admin ingress, radio/SIP alerting, AV resiliency,
and evidence capture integrations (manual export fallback when forwarding unavailable).

## 4. Interfaces

Integrated with access portal/kiosk (3‑factor), DCIM, SIEM, ticketing, and crisis communications workflows.

## 5. Drawings & Inventory

Include floor plans, rack elevations, cabling, and equipment inventories; maintain under change control.
