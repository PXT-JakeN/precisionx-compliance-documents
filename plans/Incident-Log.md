## 1. Purpose

Maintain a centralized record of security incidents, severities, actions, and outcomes to support response,
reporting, and improvement.

## 2. Required Fields

Incident ID; date/time; severity; description; assets/customers affected; root cause; containment/eradication/recovery
actions; notifications (legal/privacy/customer); PIR results; CAPA; evidence link.

## 3. Maintenance

Entries open via IR process; status tracked to closure; export artifacts if SIEM/case platform cannot forward to the
repository.
