## 1. Purpose

Track facility energy consumption and Power Usage Effectiveness (PUE) to inform capacity, resilience, and
environmental targets.

## 2. Required Fields

Site; period; IT load; total facility load; PUE; anomalies/events; maintenance impacts; improvement actions; evidence
links. Export data manually if DCIM cannot forward.

## 3. Cadence

Monthly per site with quarterly roll‑ups; align with DCIM polling intervals and telemetry thresholds.
