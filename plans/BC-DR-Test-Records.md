## 1. Purpose

Record outcomes of continuity and disaster recovery exercises, including objectives, results, and improvements.

## 2. Required Fields

Exercise ID; scenario/system; objectives; RTO/RPO targets vs. achieved; participants; outcomes; issues; CAPA; evidence
links (screenshots/logs/exports); next steps.

## 3. Maintenance

Log tabletop, restore tests, and failover events; compile quarterly summaries for management review; export evidence if
tools cannot forward artifacts to the repository.
