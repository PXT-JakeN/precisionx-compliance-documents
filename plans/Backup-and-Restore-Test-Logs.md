## 1. Purpose

Record results of backup integrity and restore tests for critical systems and workstations (iDrive360 endpoints).

## 2. Required Fields

Test ID; system/endpoint; backup type/location/immutability; test date; data set; restore steps; duration; pass/fail;
issues; CAPA; evidence link. Note manual evidence exports if backup platform cannot forward artifacts.

## 3. Cadence

Quarterly restore tests for critical data, per standard; ad‑hoc after incidents or major changes.
