## 1. Purpose

Define the program for establishing and maintaining compliance across Powered by GridSite franchises and GridColo Inside
locations.

## 2. Scope and Controls

Applies to all franchise sites and corporate‑owned/licensed data centers. Enforce technical baseline (S20), physical
security (S17), NOC build expectations (S15), and portal/kiosk access management with 3‑factor authentication,
enrollment and background checks, escort limits, guard terminal fallback, and auditable logs.

## 3. Lifecycle

1. Onboarding: Assess site readiness; deploy portal/kiosk; connect secure tunnels; validate wired workstation priority
   and UniFi Identity for Wi‑Fi; register backup endpoints; establish monitoring and evidence exports.
2. Operation: Quarterly self‑attestations and telemetry checks; semiannual compliance audits per PR33; remediate gaps.
3. Change and Expansion: Apply change control for material updates; re‑assess capacity, security, and staffing.

## 4. Reporting and Metrics

Compliance scorecards, audit findings closure, incident and access anomalies, and evidence completeness.
