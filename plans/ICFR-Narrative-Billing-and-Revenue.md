## 1. Process Overview

Narrative of order‑to‑cash: contract/order intake, usage/event capture, rating, invoicing, adjustments/credits,
collections, and revenue recognition.

## 2. Systems & Boundaries

Contract/CRM, usage metering, billing engine, GL/ERP, and data warehouse; interfaces and reconciliations defined; SSO
and MFA enforced for financial systems per Access Control Policy.

## 3. Key ICFR Controls

Maker‑checker for invoices; price/discount approvals; usage reconciliation to invoice; revenue/deferred revenue
reconciliations; change control for billing logic; segregation of duties; access reviews; evidence exports when
forwarding is unavailable.

## 4. Reports & Records

Invoice logs, adjustments, reconciliation workpapers, and audit trails retained per retention policy.

## 5. Risks & Mitigations

Improper revenue recognition, unauthorized adjustments, metering gaps, and integration failures; mitigated via
automated checks, approvals, reconciliations, and monitoring.
