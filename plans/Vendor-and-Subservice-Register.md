## 1. Purpose

Maintain an authoritative list of third parties, services provided, risk tiers, contracts, and assurance results.

## 2. Required Fields

Vendor name; service; data processed; classification; locations; risk tier; contract/NDA; SOC/ISO attestations;
subprocessor chain; security contacts; monitoring cadence; issues/CAPA; termination/exit plan.

## 3. Maintenance

Populate on onboarding; review at least annually; update after assessments/incidents; export evidence when platforms
cannot forward.
