## 1. Purpose

Track audit nonconformities, observations, corrective and preventive actions (CAPA), and closure verification.

## 2. Required Fields

Finding ID; audit ref/scope; severity; description; root cause; CAPA owner; actions; due date; status; verification
evidence; linkage to risk/SoA if control scope changes.

## 3. Maintenance

Update as audits progress; review CAPA weekly until closure; export reports if workpaper systems cannot forward.
