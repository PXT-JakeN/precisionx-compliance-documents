## 1. Purpose

Track contractual and public commitments (uptime, response, RPO/RTO, security obligations) to drive operations and
reporting.

## 2. Required Fields

Customer/segment; commitment type; metric/target; measurement source; window and exclusions; reporting cadence;
penalties/credits; owner; related runbooks/playbooks; evidence location.

## 3. Maintenance

Update upon new contracts or changes; reconcile with capacity/availability reports; export evidence if systems cannot
forward artifacts to the repository.
