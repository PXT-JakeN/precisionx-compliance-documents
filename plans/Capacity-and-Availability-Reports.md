## 1. Purpose

Provide periodic reports on capacity utilization, performance SLOs, and availability vs. commitments.

## 2. Required Fields

Service/system; period; SLO targets vs. actuals; utilization (CPU/mem/storage/network); incidents impacting uptime;
capacity risks; planned upgrades; evidence links. Export artifacts manually if telemetry cannot forward to repository.

## 3. Cadence

Monthly rollups with weekly internal dashboards; quarterly summaries for management review and customer reports as
applicable.
