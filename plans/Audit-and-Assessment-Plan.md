## 1. Objectives

Plan internal audits, supplier assessments, and external certification activities (ISO Stage 1/2, SOC readiness
and examinations) with evidence preparation and timelines.

## 2. Scope

ISMS/BCMS controls, SOC systems, core suppliers, and data centers.

## 3. Calendar & Cadence

Define quarterly internal audit themes; annual supplier assessments by tier; annual ISO surveillance post‑cert;
SOC Type 2 operating period schedule. Maintain a rolling 12‑month calendar.

## 4. Methods

Use interviews, sampling, and technical verification. Capture objective evidence, identify nonconformities, and
track corrective actions through CAPA. Coordinate with Records/Evidence policy.

## 5. Roles & Responsibilities

ISMS Manager coordinates schedules and reporting; process owners support fieldwork; executive sponsors
review outcomes and approve actions.

## 6. Reporting

Deliver audit reports with findings, severity, and due dates. Summarize status in Management Review.
