## 1. Purpose

Catalog processing activities, data categories, systems, vendors, locations, and retention to meet privacy and
contractual obligations and to inform DPIAs.

## 2. Required Fields

Processing activity; purpose; legal basis/contract; data subjects; data categories and classification; systems and
locations (region/country); vendors/subprocessors; transfers; retention; safeguards (encryption/MFA/access controls);
links to DPIA and incidents; last review date.

## 3. Maintenance

System/Data Owners update before go‑live and upon change; Privacy reviews quarterly; export reports if platforms cannot
forward evidence to the repository.
