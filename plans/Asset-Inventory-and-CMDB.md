## 1. Purpose

Maintain a complete, accurate, and timely inventory of information assets and configuration items to support security,
operations, and compliance.

## 2. Scope and Fields

Assets include endpoints, servers/VMs, containers, network devices, SaaS tenants, databases, applications, secrets,
and facilities devices. Minimum fields: unique ID; owner; custodian; classification (P06); location; environment
(prod/non‑prod); configuration baseline; dependencies; criticality; backup target; logging status; support dates.

## 3. Discovery and Updates

Automate cloud/resource discovery; integrate with CI/CD and endpoint management; require updates via change control;
review critical items quarterly; export evidence if systems cannot forward.

## 4. Responsibilities and Reviews

Owners validate entries; IT Ops maintains CMDB platform; Internal Audit and ISMS sample for completeness and accuracy
semiannually.
