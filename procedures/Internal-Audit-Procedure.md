## 1. Purpose

Define risk‑based internal audits to assess effectiveness of the ISMS/BCMS and related processes.

## 2. Roles

Internal Auditor(s), Process Owners, ISMS, Management.

## 3. Procedure

1. Plan: Develop annual risk‑based audit plan; define scope, criteria, and independence.
2. Prepare: Request evidence; schedule interviews and walkthroughs; sample populations.
3. Perform: Test design and operating effectiveness; record evidence; note nonconformities and observations.
4. Report: Issue findings with severity, root cause, and recommendations; agree owners and due dates.
5. Follow‑Up: Track CAPA to closure; verify remediation; update risk and SoA if applicable.

## 4. Records & Evidence

Audit plan, workpapers, samples, findings, CAPA, closure verification.

## 5. Metrics

On‑time audits, repeat findings rate, CAPA closure time, audit coverage.
