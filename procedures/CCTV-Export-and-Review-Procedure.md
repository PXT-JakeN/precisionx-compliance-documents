## 1. Purpose

Define how CCTV footage is requested, exported, preserved, reviewed, and shared with authorized parties.

## 2. Roles

Requester, Facilities (exporter), Security/IR, Legal, Site Manager, ISMS.

## 3. Procedure

1. Request: Submit ticket with incident details (time range, cameras, reason); obtain approvals (Facilities + Legal
   for external disclosure).
2. Export: Use system tools to export with hash/metadata; include watermark/time overlays; maintain chain of custody.
3. Preserve: Store in evidence repository; limit access; retain per policy or legal hold.
4. Review: Conduct in controlled setting; log viewers; document findings and timestamps; escalate to IR if needed.
5. Share: Provide copies using secure transfer; redact where necessary; record disclosures.

## 4. Records & Evidence

Requests, approvals, export logs, hashes, custody records, review notes, disclosure logs.

## 5. Metrics

Export turnaround time, completeness of custody records, review-to-closure time.
