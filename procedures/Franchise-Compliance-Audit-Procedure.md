## 1. Purpose

Define the audit approach for verifying franchise sites meet technical, security, and operational baselines.

## 2. Roles

Audit Team, Site Manager, Facilities, NOC Representative, ISMS.

## 3. Prerequisites

Audit checklist, last evidence package, prior findings, site access coordination, safety briefing.

## 4. Procedure

1. Plan: Confirm scope and sampling; request evidence (access logs, CCTV retention, maintenance records, training).
2. Conduct: Inspect physical controls (portal/kiosk, guard terminal, escort limits), network segmentation, NOC link,
   DCIM telemetry, and security records; interview personnel.
3. Rate Findings: Classify severity and owners; agree on due dates and remediation plans.
4. Report & Track: Issue report; log CAPA items; require evidence of closure; schedule re‑audit for major issues.

## 5. Records & Evidence

Audit plan, evidence package, findings report, CAPA tracker, closure proofs.

## 6. Metrics

Open findings aging, repeat findings rate, on‑time remediation.
