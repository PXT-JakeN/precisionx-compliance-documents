## 1. Purpose

Define the planning, approval, execution, and rollback steps for MEP activities affecting facilities MEP systems.

## 2. Roles

MEP Coordinator, Facilities Engineer, Vendor, NOC Representative, Site Manager, EHS.

## 3. Procedure

1. Plan: Draft MOP with scope, time, methods, safety, rollback, and communications; review telemetry impacts.
2. Approve: Obtain approvals (Facilities, EHS, NOC, ISMS for risk); schedule during maintenance window.
3. Execute: Conduct pre‑job brief; implement steps; maintain radio/SIP/Teams comms with NOC; capture readings.
4. Monitor: Track DCIM telemetry and alarms; abort if risk thresholds exceeded; notify stakeholders.
5. Rollback/Close: Roll back if needed; validate normal operations; file MOP, logs, photos; record deviations and CAPA.

## 4. Records & Evidence

Approved MOP, checklists, telemetry snapshots, approvals, completion report.

## 5. Metrics

Change success rate, rollback rate, incidents during MEP, telemetry deviation counts.
