## 1. Purpose

Operationalize the planning and rollout of the ISMS across GridSite, ensuring defined scope, risk management,
control selection, evidence collection, and continual improvement.

## 2. Roles

ISMS Manager (lead), Control Owners, GRC Analyst, Security Engineering, IT Operations, Facilities, Legal, Executives.

## 3. Prerequisites

Approved ISMS scope and context; risk criteria; WRITING-PLAN baseline; document templates; evidence repository.

## 4. Procedure

1. Establish Scope & Governance: Confirm scope boundaries, interested parties, and governing committees.
2. Risk Framework: Validate risk methodology and appetite; seed risk register; schedule assessments.
3. Control Selection: Draft SoA; map controls to owners; define metrics and evidence per control.
4. Documentation: Populate policies/standards/procedures per WRITING-PLAN; route for review and approval.
5. Technical Controls: Implement identity (M365/Entra SSO/MFA; Duo for SSH/RDP), logging/time sync, backups,
   vulnerability scanning, segmentation, and workstation/server baselines in cloud‑first environments (Linode/DigitalOcean).
6. Evidence & Records: Centralize approvals, configurations, logs; for systems lacking forwarding, export manually on cadence.
7. Training & Awareness: Roll out required modules; track completion and effectiveness.
8. Monitoring & Improvement: Launch metrics dashboards; run internal audits; manage exceptions; conduct management reviews.
9. External Readiness: Prepare ISO Stage 1 and SOC readiness; address gaps; maintain audit packs.

## 5. Records & Evidence

SoA, risk register, approvals, change records, training logs, control metrics, audit findings, and CAPA.

## 6. Metrics

Policy coverage, control evidence completeness, audit issue closure time, and objective attainment.
