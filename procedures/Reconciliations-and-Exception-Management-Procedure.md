## 1. Purpose

Standardize financial reconciliations and the lifecycle of exceptions from identification to closure.

## 2. Roles

Preparer, Reviewer (independent), Finance Controller, Process Owners, Internal Audit.

## 3. Procedure

1. Define Recons: Maintain a catalog covering bank, AR/AP, revenue/deferred revenue, usage‑to‑invoice, sub‑ledger to
   GL; assign owners and frequency.
2. Prepare: Reconcile per schedule; explain variances with supporting evidence; flag aging items over thresholds.
3. Review: Independent reviewer validates methods and evidence; approve or return; record review sign‑off.
4. Exceptions: Log exceptions with cause, risk, and action; set due dates; escalate overdue; consider compensating
   controls and document per exception procedure.
5. Close: Resolve and document closure; reflect adjustments in the next cycle; archive workpapers (manual export if
   forwarding is unavailable).

## 4. Records & Evidence

Reconciliation workpapers, approvals, exception logs, closure evidence, adjustments.

## 5. Metrics

On‑time completion, aged exceptions, variance trend, review return rate.
