## 1. Purpose

Define the cadence and method for producing ISMS/BCMS performance and risk reports for management review.

## 2. Roles

ISMS (owner), Control Owners, SecOps/SRE, BC/DR Lead, Executives.

## 3. Procedure

1. Select Metrics: Align to objectives and top risks; include KRIs and KPIs (e.g., incident MTTR, vuln SLA, backup
   success, access review closure, capacity SLOs).
2. Compile: Pull data from CCM and source systems; verify integrity; annotate context and exceptions.
3. Publish: Issue monthly and quarterly packs; distribute to leadership; store artifacts in evidence; export manually
   if forwarding is not possible.
4. Review: Capture decisions and actions from management reviews; log CAPA.
5. Track: Monitor trends and target attainment; propose adjustments to objectives and measures.

## 4. Records & Evidence

Metric packs, raw extracts, review minutes, CAPA trackers.

## 5. Metrics

On‑time reporting, completeness, action closure rates, trend coverage.
