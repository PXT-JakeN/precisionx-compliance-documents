## 1. Purpose

Define how GridSite plans, conducts, evaluates, and improves tabletop exercises for incident and continuity
scenarios.

## 2. Roles

Facilitator, Scribe, Participants (Ops, SecOps, SRE, Facilities, Legal, Comms), Observers, ISMS.

## 3. Prerequisites

Exercise charter with objectives, scope, and success criteria; selected scenario(s); participant list; logistics and
collaboration channels (e.g., Microsoft Teams with live AV for NOC linkage).

## 4. Procedure

1. Plan: Select scenario(s) tied to top risks (e.g., cloud outage, ransomware, facility event); define injects and
   evaluation criteria; prepare artifacts (diagrams, RTO/RPO targets, contact lists).
2. Conduct: Kickoff, ground rules, and timeboxing; deliver injects; facilitate discussion; record decisions, gaps,
   and timing.
3. Evaluate: Score against objectives; capture strengths, issues, and improvement items; assign owners and due dates.
4. Improve: Track CAPA to closure; update playbooks, plans, and training; schedule follow-up drills if needed.

## 5. Records & Evidence

Agenda, attendance, inject deck, notes, decisions, CAPA tracker, updated artifacts.

## 6. Metrics

Objective attainment, time-to-decision, number of issues identified/closed, and follow-up completion rate.
