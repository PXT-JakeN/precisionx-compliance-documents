## 1. Purpose

Coordinate investigation and notification when personal or confidential customer information is exposed or
exfiltrated.

## 2. Actions

1. Confirm & Scope: Validate indicators; identify data types, systems, tenants, and affected jurisdictions.
2. Contain: Disable compromised accounts; isolate systems; preserve evidence; prevent further egress.
3. Assess Obligations: Engage Legal to determine breach definitions and notification timelines per contracts/law.
4. Notify: Coordinate external communications per Incident Communications & PR Policy; inform customers and
   authorities as required.
5. Remediate: Patch flaws; rotate credentials; strengthen controls; monitor for recurrence.
6. PIR/CAPA: Root‑cause and track remediations; improve detections and training.

## 3. Records

Incident timeline, data inventories, notification lists, communications, corrective actions.
