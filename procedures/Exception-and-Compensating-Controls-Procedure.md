## 1. Purpose

Define the process to request, assess, approve, monitor, and retire exceptions and compensating controls.

## 2. Roles

Requester, Control Owner, ISMS, Risk Committee, Executive Sponsor (for high risk).

## 3. Procedure

1. Request: Submit description, affected controls, risk analysis, proposed compensating controls, owner, expiry, and
   monitoring plan.
2. Review: Control Owner and ISMS assess risk and viability; require executive approval for high risk/long duration.
3. Record: Log in exception register; update SoA if Annex A control impacted; define evidence requirements.
4. Monitor: Review at least quarterly; collect evidence; adjust or revoke if conditions change.
5. Retire: Implement remediation; verify control effective; close register entry and archive records.

## 4. Records & Evidence

Exception requests/approvals, risk assessments, monitoring results, closure proof.

## 5. Metrics

Open exceptions by risk, overdue reviews, time‑to‑remediate, residual risk trend.
