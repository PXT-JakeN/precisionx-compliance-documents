## 1. Purpose

Define steps for collecting, reviewing, and approving vendor security due diligence prior to granting access or
processing data.

## 2. Scope

All new suppliers and material changes to existing suppliers.

## 3. Procedure

1. Initiation
   - Open onboarding ticket; assign risk tier based on data, access, and business impact.
2. Questionnaire & Evidence
   - Send tailored questionnaire; collect SOC reports, policies, and technical controls; verify NDAs/contracts.
3. Review & Findings
   - Assess against GridSite requirements; document gaps and required mitigations with due dates.
4. Decision
   - Approve, approve with conditions, or reject; record approvals and conditions in vendor record.

6. Handover to Monitoring
