## 1. Purpose

Define intake, triage, and escalation paths for customer issues to ensure timely resolution and proper
communication.

## 2. Roles

Support Tier 1/2, NOC, On‑Call Engineering, SecOps, Account Manager, Communications.

## 3. Prerequisites

Ticketing system with priority/severity matrix, on‑call schedules, status page access, Microsoft Teams live NOC link.

## 4. Procedure

1. Intake & Triage: Classify severity (S1–S4) and impact; acknowledge per SLA; record details and evidence.
2. Escalate: Page appropriate on‑call (SRE, Engineering, SecOps); coordinate via NOC bridge.
3. Communicate: Post updates to ticket and status page (for S1/S2); follow Incident Communications & PR Policy.
4. Resolve & Verify: Implement fix/workaround; validate with monitoring and customer confirmation.
5. Close & Learn: Document root cause and actions; link to PIR/CAPA for S1/S2; export artifacts if logging cannot
   be forwarded.

## 5. Records & Evidence

Tickets, timelines, customer communications, monitoring snapshots, PIR/CAPA items.

## 6. Metrics

Response/resolve SLAs, reopen rate, customer satisfaction (CSAT), S1/S2 frequency.
