## 1. Purpose

Provide step‑by‑step guidance to control hazardous energy during servicing and maintenance.

## 2. Roles

Authorized Employee, Affected Employee, Supervisor, EHS.

## 3. Procedure

1. Prepare: Identify energy sources; notify affected employees; review procedures and equipment‑specific diagrams.
2. Shutdown: Power down equipment; isolate all energy sources (electrical, mechanical, pneumatic, hydraulic).
3. Lock & Tag: Apply locks and tags at isolation points; verify each worker’s personal lock/tag; document.
4. Verify Zero Energy: Test controls; attempt start to verify isolation; return to safe state.
5. Perform Work: Maintain lock/tag control; prohibit bypassing; maintain keys with authorized employees.
6. Restore: Remove tools; ensure guards in place; clear personnel; remove locks/tags in reverse order; notify
   stakeholders.

## 4. Records & Evidence

LOTO logs, training records, equipment‑specific procedures, inspection checklists.

## 5. Metrics

LOTO inspection pass rate, incident/near‑miss counts, training completion.
