## 1. Purpose

Define how employees and contractors report environmental, health, and safety incidents and how EHS triages,
investigates, and closes them.

## 2. Roles

Reporter, Supervisor, EHS Lead, Facilities, Security (as needed), ISMS.

## 3. Procedure

1. Report: Immediately report incidents/near misses via EHS form or hotline; seek medical aid first when needed.
2. Triage: Classify severity; secure area; preserve evidence; notify stakeholders; open corrective action items.
3. Investigate: Collect statements, photos, and logs; review LOTO adherence if applicable.
4. Correct & Prevent: Implement corrective actions; assign owners and due dates; track to closure.
5. Communicate: Share lessons learned and any policy changes with affected teams.

## 4. Records & Evidence

Incident report, photos, statements, corrective actions, closure evidence.

## 5. Metrics

Incident rate, time‑to‑report, corrective action closure time, repeat incident rate.
