## 1. Purpose

Operationalize retention schedules and secure destruction for records and data in line with policy, contracts, and
law.

## 2. Roles

Record Owners, System Owners, Legal, ISMS, IT Operations.

## 3. Prerequisites

Published retention schedules; legal hold register; classification and residency metadata; disposal vendors and
methods approved.

## 4. Procedure

1. Identify: Query repositories for items at or beyond retention; include backups, logs, exports, and media.
2. Verify Holds: Check legal/regulatory holds; exclude held items; document decisions.
3. Approve: Obtain owner and Legal approval for destruction batch; generate destruction plan.
4. Destroy: Execute approved method (crypto‑erase, secure wipe, shredding); for cloud, leverage provider features;
   for paper/media, use approved vendors.
5. Evidence: Capture certificates of destruction, logs, and sample hashes; where forwarding is not possible, export
   artifacts manually to evidence store.
6. Update: Mark items disposed; update inventories and retention reports.

## 5. Records & Evidence

Retention queries, hold checks, approvals, certificates of destruction, audit logs.

## 6. Metrics

On‑time disposal rate, exceptions due to holds, audit sample pass rate.
