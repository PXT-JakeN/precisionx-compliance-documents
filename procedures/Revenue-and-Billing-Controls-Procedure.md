## 1. Purpose

Define ICFR‑relevant controls for revenue recognition and billing accuracy, completeness, and timeliness.

## 2. Roles

Finance Controller (owner), Billing Operations, Sales Ops, Engineering/SaaS Ops, Internal Audit.

## 3. Procedure

1. Contract & Order Intake: Validate executed agreements and pricing; require approvals for discounts; record in
   source systems with unique IDs.
2. Usage & Event Capture: Reconcile metering/events to billing; protect integrity via access controls and logs;
   export evidence if systems cannot forward.
3. Invoicing: Generate invoices with maker‑checker review; enforce cutoffs; retain signed PDFs and transmission logs.
4. Adjustments & Credits: Require documented approval workflows; track reasons and linkage to original invoices.
5. Reconciliation: Perform monthly revenue and deferred revenue reconciliations; investigate variances; document
   resolution.

## 4. Records & Evidence

Contracts, order forms, usage exports, invoice logs, approvals, reconciliation workpapers.

## 5. Metrics

Billing error rate, credit/adjustment rate, reconciliation timeliness, aged variances.
