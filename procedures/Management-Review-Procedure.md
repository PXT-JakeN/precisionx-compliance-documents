## 1. Purpose

Define the cadence, inputs, agenda, and outputs for executive management reviews of the ISMS/BCMS.

## 2. Roles

Executive Sponsor (chair), ISMS Manager, Control Owners, BC/DR Lead, Security Operations Lead, Process Owners.

## 3. Procedure

1. Schedule & Inputs: Hold quarterly reviews (minimum semi‑annual). Inputs include objectives progress, metrics/KRIs,
   audit results, incidents, vulnerabilities/patch, exceptions, supplier risks, legal/regulatory changes, capacity
   and continuity outcomes.
2. Review & Decide: Evaluate performance against targets and risk appetite; confirm resourcing; approve corrective
   and preventive actions (CAPA); endorse changes to scope, policies, SoA, and objectives.
3. Actions & Records: Assign owners and due dates; log decisions and CAPA; store packs and minutes in evidence
   repository. If systems cannot forward artifacts, export and archive manually.

## 4. Records & Evidence

Agenda, attendee list, metric packs, minutes, decisions, CAPA tracker updates.

## 5. Metrics

On‑time reviews, action closure rate, objectives attainment, high‑risk exceptions trend.
