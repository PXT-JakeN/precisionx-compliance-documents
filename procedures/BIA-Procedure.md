## 1. Purpose

Define steps to identify critical processes, dependencies, impacts, and recovery targets (RTO/RPO) for GridSite
functions and services.

## 2. Roles

BC/DR Lead (owner), Process Owners, System Owners, GRC Analyst, ISMS Manager.

## 3. Prerequisites

Catalog of services and processes; dependency maps; data classification and residency constraints.

## 4. Procedure

1. Scoping: Select functions/services; identify stakeholders and peak periods.
2. Data Collection: Use standardized BIA questionnaires to capture inputs, outputs, dependencies (people, tech,
   facilities, vendors), and regulatory/contractual constraints.
3. Impact & Tolerances: Quantify financial, operational, legal, and reputational impacts over time; propose RTO/RPO.
4. Validation: Review results with owners; reconcile against customer SLAs and capacity model.
5. Prioritization: Assign criticality tiers; feed requirements into DR plans and capacity planning.
6. Documentation: Publish BIA reports; update dependency and data residency inventories.
7. Review Cadence: Revisit at least annually and after significant changes.

## 5. Records & Evidence

BIA questionnaires, validated RTO/RPO, dependency matrices, sign‑offs.

## 6. Metrics

Coverage of critical functions, freshness of BIAs, and alignment gaps resolved.
