## 1. Purpose

Define monitoring steps for subservice organizations, including intake of reports, review of gaps, and tracking
of mitigations and CUECs.

## 2. Scope

All subservice organizations supporting GridSite services.

## 3. Procedure

1. Evidence Intake
   - Collect SOC reports/certifications and security updates at least annually and upon significant changes.
2. Gap Analysis
   - Identify control gaps and required CUECs; document mitigations and owner actions.
3. Risk Update
   - Update supplier risk tier and treatment plans; escalate material issues to Management Review.
4. Access Review
   - Verify least‑privilege access for subservice accounts; remove stale access.
5. Recordkeeping
   - Maintain monitoring records and approvals; schedule next review.

## 4. Records

Reports, analyses, action items, approvals, and next‑review dates.
