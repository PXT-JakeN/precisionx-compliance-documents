## Instructions

Complete all sections. Attach requested evidence (SOC reports, ISO certs, pen‑test summaries, policies). Provide
accurate and current responses; indicate N/A where appropriate.

## Section 1 – Vendor Profile

Name, address, primary contacts, services provided, subprocessor use, data processed (types, volumes), regions.

## Section 2 – Compliance & Governance

Attestations (ISO 27001, SOC 2, PCI, HIPAA, etc.), audit history, privacy program, DPO contact, breach notification
commitments.

## Section 3 – Security Controls

Identity/MFA, network security, vulnerability management cadence, patch timelines, logging/SIEM, encryption at rest/in
transit, key management, secrets handling, backups/immutability, incident response.

## Section 4 – Application/Cloud Security

SDLC practices, SAST/DAST, dependency management, CI/CD security, tenant isolation (if multi‑tenant), API security.

## Section 5 – Business Continuity & DR

BC/DR capabilities, RTO/RPO commitments, exercise cadence, evidence of recent tests.

## Section 6 – Data Protection & Privacy

Data classification, retention, deletion, DSAR handling, cross‑border transfers and safeguards.

## Section 7 – Incident History & Insurance

Security incidents in last 24 months (summary), remedial actions, cyber insurance coverage.

## Section 8 – Documents Provided (Checklist)

- [ ] SOC 2 report(s) and bridge letter
- [ ] ISO certificates
- [ ] Pen‑test executive summary
- [ ] Policy set (security, privacy, IR)
- [ ] BC/DR test results
