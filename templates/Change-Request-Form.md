## Purpose

Use this form to request, assess, approve, implement, and review changes per policy and procedures.

## Instructions

- Complete all required fields. Attach supporting evidence (plans, test results, rollbacks).
- For emergency changes, submit within 24 hours post‑implementation.
- Link to related tickets and impacted assets/config items.

## Section B – Description, Rationale, and Risk

Provide a concise description, business justification, and expected benefits. Document risk rating and impact analysis
(availability, security, compliance). Note any customer commitments (SLAs) potentially affected.

## Section D – Backout/Rollback Plan

Define backout steps, success/abort criteria, and data recovery considerations.

## Section E – Validation & Evidence

Describe pre‑/post‑change tests, monitoring, and acceptance criteria. Attach evidence. If platforms cannot forward
artifacts, export and store in the evidence repository.
