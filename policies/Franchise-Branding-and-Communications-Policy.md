## 1. Purpose

Ensure consistent, authorized use of GridSite and franchise brands and establish requirements for external and
customer communications.

## 2. Scope

All brand usage and communications by GridSite and franchise operators, including public websites, social media,
sales collateral, and customer notices.

## 3. Policy Statements

Brand assets shall be used only as provided in official guidelines; modifications require Marketing approval.
External communications must be accurate, use appropriate data classification labels, and avoid disclosure of
INTERNAL or RESTRICTED information. Incident‑related communications follow the Incident Communications & PR
Policy. Franchise operators must clearly represent the Powered by GridSite relationship and comply with
co‑branding and disclosure standards. Records of approvals and published materials are retained per policy.
Unauthorised use or misrepresentation will result in corrective actions up to franchise sanction.

## 4. Exceptions

Exceptions require Marketing and Legal approval.

## 5. Compliance Measurement

Measured via approval logs, content reviews, and periodic audits of public channels.
