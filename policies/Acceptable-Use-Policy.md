## 1. Purpose

Set expectations for acceptable use of GridSite systems, data, and communication platforms.

## 2. Scope

All employees, contractors, vendors, and franchise personnel using GridSite resources.

## 3. Policy Statements

Users must access only information and systems required for their role and handle data per classification.
Company data may not be stored on personal cloud/storage. Unauthorized software, malicious tools, and
bypass of security controls are prohibited. Strong authentication and MFA must be used where required.
Company communication platforms must be used for business communications; users must avoid phishing and
report suspicious activity. Remote work must follow the Remote Work Policy. Monitoring for compliance may
occur consistent with law and policy.

## 4. Exceptions

Exceptions require ISMS approval.

## 5. Compliance Measurement

Measured via periodic audits and incident reports; violations may lead to disciplinary action.
