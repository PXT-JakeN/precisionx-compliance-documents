## 1. Purpose

Establish requirements for role‑based training, awareness, and phishing exercises to build and sustain a
security‑first culture.

## 2. Scope

All employees and contractors; specialized modules for developers, admins, and NOC/facility personnel.

## 3. Policy Statements

Onboarding training is mandatory prior to system access and is refreshed at least annually. Role‑based modules
cover topics such as data classification and handling, remote work, privileged access, incident reporting, and
secure development. Recurring phishing simulations are conducted with feedback and targeted coaching. 
Completion and results are recorded and reported in Management Review.

## 4. Exceptions

Exceptions require ISMS approval.

## 5. Compliance Measurement

Measured via completion rates, phishing metrics, and corrective actions.
